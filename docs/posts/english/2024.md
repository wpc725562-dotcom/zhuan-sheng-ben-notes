## 写作（必背）

**题目方向：** 假定你是李华。你校英文报面向交换生举办主题为 “China in My Eyes” 的写作比赛，请给交换生 Mark 写一封邮件邀请他参赛。需包含：比赛主题与对象；字数要求（500 词内）；截止日期（11 月 10 日）；鼓励参赛。

### 参考范文

```
Dear Mark,

I'm Li Hua. Our school English newspaper is holding a writing contest named "China in My Eyes" for exchange students. You need to write about your view of China within 500 words. The deadline is November 10th. It's a great chance to share your thoughts. I believe you have unique insights. So come and join it!

Best regards,
Li Hua
```

**得分点：** 格式正确 · 要点齐全 · 约 100 词 · 少语法硬伤

### 审题清单
- [ ] 体裁（书信 / NOTICE）与称呼落款
- [ ] 时间 / 地点 / 对象 / 目的 信息齐全
- [ ] 90–120 词，分段清楚
- [ ] 无中式英语硬伤

详细解析：
1、B。根据 Organic EcoPark 部分 “such as playing on a bouncy castle,
riding horses, feeding fish, picking vegetables and mushrooms” 可知，在
Organic EcoPark 可以骑马，答案选 B。
2、A。根据 Go Green Farm 部分 “And it is pet - friendly. Your four -
legged friends can have a good time there.” 可知，Go Green Farm 适合有狗的
人，答案选 A。
3、D。根据 Sunrise Village 部分 “you will get a chance to know the entire
rice production process. It will make your trip special.” 可知，了解水稻生
产过程让 Sunrise Village 的旅行变得特别，答案选 D。
4、C。根据 Butterfly Valley 部分 “The farm doesn't take walk - ins, so you
need to make a reservation before heading out there.” 可知，去 Butterfly
Valley 之前需要提前预订，答案选 C。
5、B。根据 Holiday Farm 部分 “Admission:$30(adults). $20 (kids)”，一个家
长和两个孩子的费用是 30 + 20×2 = 70 美元，答案选 B。
6、C。根据 “The lack of windows does affect students’ mental health, as
research shows. It makes their concentration span (持续时间) shorter.” 可
知，没有窗户会缩短学生的注意力持续时间，答案选 C。
7、D。根据 “Hayden took all the photos of trees himself. Then he got them
printed onto the ceilings.” 可知，Hayden 把树的照片印在天花板上，将自然带
入教室，答案选 D。
8、B。根据 “Overall, the class attendance has gone up because students are
happier and want to come in more frequently” 可知，教室的变化使学生的出勤
率提高了，答案选 B。
9、A。根据 “A student called Dave said that the new addition did bring him
peace. ‘It is surprising to see trees here. It is pretty great and
beautiful. When I look up, it feels like I am sitting under a tree,’ Dave
said.” 可知，Dave 对新变化持赞成态度，答案选 A。
10、D。文章主要讲述了 Philip Hayden 发现无窗教室对学生的不良影响后，通过在
天花板印树的照片来改善教室环境，结果学生更快乐、出勤率提高，对学生表现有积
极影响，答案选 D。
11、B。根据 “Birds gained the ability to fly about 150 million years ago,
which was a major event in animal history.” 可知，动物历史上的一个重大事件
是鸟类获得了飞行能力，答案选 B。
12、A。第二段主要讲述了科学家对现代鸟类进行脑部扫描，观察它们休息和飞行 10
分钟后的大脑活动，这是研究的关键过程，答案选 A。
13、D。根据 “To determine exactly how birds started to fly, the research
team needed to figure out what the cerebellum looked like in creatures that
were alive around the time when birds began to fly. They studied a group
of bird - like dinosaurs...” 可知，研究人员研究类鸟恐龙是为了弄清楚它们的
小脑是什么样的，答案选 D。
14、C。根据 “The researchers couldn't look at the actual brains because
those deteriorated long ago. But they could look at the dinosaurs’ skulls
(颅骨), which once held the brains.” 可知，由于大脑很久以前就损坏了，研究
人员无法查看实际的大脑，deteriorated 意为 “被损坏”，答案选 C。
15、C。文章主要讲述了科学家对鸟类飞行能力与小脑关系的研究，属于科学研究类内
容，最有可能出现在杂志的科学板块，答案选 C。
