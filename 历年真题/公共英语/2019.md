## 写作

**题目方向：** 邀请外国朋友 Peter 参加学校「中国传统文化展」，写邀请信。

**参考范文：**

```
Dear Peter,

I am writing to invite you to the Traditional Chinese Culture Exhibition
in our school.
First of all, it will be held in the school hall at 2:00 p.m. next Friday.
In addition, you can try paper-cutting and watch Chinese calligraphy.
What is more, it will help you better understand Chinese culture.
I sincerely hope you can come. I am looking forward to your early reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
```

**得分点：** 目的一句清；时间+体验细节；落款 Li Hua。
**默写自检：** [ ] 称呼落款 [ ] 目的 [ ] 三点细节 [ ] 盼复
