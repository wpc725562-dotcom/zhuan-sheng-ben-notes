# 公共英语 · 2020 年真题演练

> 资料层级：B/C（同型演练 + 得分讲解）| 见 [[00-资料来源与使用说明]]

## 写作

**题目方向：** 申请学生会干事 / 英语社团志愿者，说明条件与能做什么。

**参考范文框架：**

```
Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to apply for the position of English club assistant.
First of all, I have passed CET-4 and speak English fluently.
In addition, I once organized an English corner and have teamwork skills.
What is more, I am patient and willing to serve other students.
I would be very grateful if you could give me an interview.
I am looking forward to your early reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
```

**得分点：** 申请目标明确；2–3 条自身条件对应岗位；礼貌请求。
