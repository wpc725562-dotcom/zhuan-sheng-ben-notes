# 公共英语 · 2018 年真题演练

> 写作体裁：**通知 NOTICE** | 约 100 词应用文
> 资料层级：B/C（同型演练 + 得分讲解）| 见 [[00-资料来源与使用说明]]

## 写作

**题目方向（活动通知）：**
学生会举办校园文化活动（音乐周/读书周等），写 NOTICE：说明时间、地点、内容，欢迎参加。

**审题要点：** NOTICE 居中；when/where/what；欢迎句；右下角 The Student Union + 日期。

**参考范文（约 100 词）：**

```
                    NOTICE

To enrich our campus life, the Student Union will hold a Music Week.
It will take place in the school hall at 2:00 p.m. next Friday.
There will be many exciting activities. For one thing, you can enjoy
pop and folk music. For another, please bring your own instruments if
you wish to perform.
Anyone who is interested is welcome to join us.
If you have any questions, please contact us at 1234567.

                                          The Student Union
                                          May 10th, 2018
```

**得分点：** 格式三分立；信息齐全；漏时间/地点直接掉内容分。
**默写自检：** [ ] 标题 [ ] 时间地点 [ ] 两项活动 [ ] 欢迎 [ ] 落款
